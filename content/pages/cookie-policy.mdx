# Cookie Policy

**Last modified:** January 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how **Antigravity Skills** ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at [antigravityskills.com](https://antigravityskills.com). It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

## 2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide reporting information.

## 3. How We Use Cookies & Consent

**Consent Principle:** When you first visit our Website, we may present you with a Cookie Banner asking for your consent to use non-essential cookies. By clicking "Accept", you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

We use cookies for several reasons:

- **Essential Cookies:** Strictly necessary for the Website to function (e.g., logging in, security checks). These cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how our Website is being used (e.g., via Google Analytics or Cloudflare) to improve performance.
- **Functionality Cookies:** Remember your preferences (like language or dark mode settings).

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use various third-party services that also place cookies on your device:

- **Security:** Cloudflare (to detect bots and malicious traffic).
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics (to analyze traffic trends).
- **Payments:** Third-party processors (e.g., Stripe) may use cookies to process transactions securely.

## 5. Your Choices & Withdrawal of Consent

You have the right to accept or reject cookies.

- **Browser Settings:** You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies.
- **Withdrawal:** If you have accepted cookies and wish to withdraw consent, you can clear your browser's cache/cookies for this domain.
- **Do Not Track:** Some browsers offer "Do Not Track" signals. We attempt to honor these signals where technically feasible.
