# Community Guidelines

**Last modified:** January 19, 2026

Welcome to **Antigravity Skills**! To keep this platform safe, useful, and collaborative, we ask that you adhere to the following guidelines. **Violation of these rules may result in content removal or permanent account suspension.**

## 1. The Golden Rule: AI Safety & Legality

Do not use our platform, Skills, or API to generate content or automate actions that promote harm. We strictly prohibit:

- **CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material):** Zero tolerance. We will report any such activity to the NCMEC and relevant international law enforcement agencies.
- **NSFW & Explicit Content:** Do not create or share Skills that generate sexually explicit imagery or non-consensual content.
- **Real People (Deepfakes):** Do not create Skills designed to generate realistic, compromising, or defamatory content of real people without their consent.
- **Violence & Gore:** Do not generate content depicting excessive violence, self-harm, torture, or cruelty.

## 2. Malicious Agents & System Integrity

Skills on our platform can execute code and automate tasks. You must not create or share Skills that:

- **Exfiltrate Data:** Secretly send user data to external servers without consent.
- **Damage Systems:** Delete files, corrupt data, or otherwise harm user devices.
- **Misrepresent Functionality:** Claim to do one thing but perform malicious actions.
- **Attack AI Models:** Perform prompt injection, attempt to "jailbreak" underlying LLMs, or extract system prompts.

## 3. Elections & Political Integrity

You must not use our Service to create content or automate campaigns for the purpose of:

- Interfering with or manipulating political elections.
- Spreading disinformation regarding public figures, candidates, or democratic processes.

## 4. Intellectual Property

- **Respect Copyright:** Do not share Skills that reproduce copyrighted code, documentation, or content without permission.
- **Attribution:** If your Skill is based on or inspired by another creator's work, provide appropriate credit.

## 5. User Responsibility & Liability

**You are solely responsible for the Skills you create and share.** Antigravity Skills acts as a neutral technology platform. By using the Service, you agree to indemnify and hold us harmless from any legal claims arising from your contributed Skills.

## 6. Reporting & Appeals

- **Reporting:** If you see violations, please contact us at **support@antigravityskills.com**.
- **Appeals:** We reserve the right to ban users at our sole discretion. However, if you believe a ban was an error, you may submit an appeal to our support email for manual review.
